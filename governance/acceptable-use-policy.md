# Acceptable Use Policy

## Allowed Use
Professional planning, documentation, analysis, sanitized examples, website delivery support, assessment support, and self-review.

## Restricted Use
Sensitive data, client-facing outputs, stakeholder-facing reports, external writes, deployments, publishing, and environment-variable changes require explicit approval.

## Forbidden Use
Do not store secrets, credentials, private data, raw client files, raw assessment files, or personally identifying records. Do not use the repo for formal education submissions, grading, or course-specific materials.

## Required Approvals
Approval is required for writes, deployments, publishing, sensitive data use, and high-risk Integration Adapter actions.
